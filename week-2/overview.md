# Week 2 — Access Control, Proofs & Patterns

## Weekly Theme
*"Master the permission system. Understand why proofs matter. Learn to think branch-free."*

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of Week 2, participants will be able to:

1. Generate and verify input proofs client-side and understand what they protect against
2. Design correct permission flows for complex multi-user contracts
3. Identify and fix all 7 common FHEVM anti-patterns
4. Write fully branch-free logic using `FHE.select` and boolean combinators
5. Implement transient cross-contract permissions with `FHE.allowTransient`
