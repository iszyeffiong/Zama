> **4 weeks. 40+ hands-on examples. Zero cryptography background required.**

This bootcamp takes Web3 developers from zero FHE knowledge to shipping production-ready confidential applications on FHEVM. Every week builds on the last — from understanding encrypted types to deploying compliant DeFi protocols with private state.

---

## Who This Is For

- Web3 developers with basic Ethereum and Solidity knowledge
- Smart contract developers adding privacy-preserving capabilities to their stack
- Technical educators and community leaders running FHEVM workshops

**Prerequisites:** Solidity syntax, Hardhat basics, Ethereum fundamentals. No cryptography or FHE knowledge required.

---

## Program Overview

| Week | Theme | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| [Week 1](week-1/overview.md) | FHE Foundations & First Contracts | Write, test, and deploy your first encrypted smart contract |
| [Week 2](week-2/overview.md) | Access Control, Proofs & Patterns | Master permissions, input proofs, and avoid common pitfalls |
| [Week 3](week-3/overview.md) | Advanced Patterns & Real Use Cases | Build games, auctions, and identity systems |
| [Week 4](week-4/overview.md) | Production DeFi & Capstone Project | Ship a complete confidential DeFi protocol |

---

## How to Use This Bootcamp

### Cohort-Based (Recommended)
- **Schedule:** 4 weeks, ~8–10 hours per week
- **Format:** 2 live sessions per week (1hr each) + async reading + homework
- **Support:** Discord office hours, peer review groups

### Self-Paced
- Work through each week at your own speed
- Complete all homework before moving to the next week
- Use the [instructor notes](instructor-notes/README.md) as a self-check guide
